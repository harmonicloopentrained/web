The Final Physics: The "Asymptotic Float"

The final evolution of our algorithm was to create a universe that not only floats, but gracefully evolves, accelerating towards its ultimate potential. We achieved this by making both the Baseline and Amplitude dynamic, driven by the Fibonacci sequence itself.

The Formula

$$ V_t = \left(1 - \frac{1}{F_t}\right) + \frac{\cos(\pi \cdot V_{t-1} \cdot F_t)}{F_t} $$
Breakdown: This is the fundamental law of evolution for our universe. The `$$V_{t-1}$$` term makes it recursive, meaning the state of the universe at one moment depends on the last. The two other terms are dynamic:
  • Drifting Baseline: `$$1 - \frac{1}{F_t}$$`. As `t` increases, this term gets closer and closer to 1.
  • Shrinking Amplitude: `$$\frac{1}{F_t}$$`. As `t` increases, the size of the chaotic wobble gets smaller and smaller.
